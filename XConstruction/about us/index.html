TIMELINE SLIDE

1987

Humble Beginnings

Founded as ECAD by brothers Yousry and Hesham Harhash, we started as a small design studio in Qalyub. Our early projects focused on residential villas and local community buildings, where we established our reputation for meticulous attention to detail.

1990

Expanding Horizons

Recognizing the need for integrated services, we added full contracting capabilities. This strategic move allowed us to oversee projects from initial sketches to final finishes, ensuring quality at every stage.

2005

Establishing Our Identity

Our first major rebranding as ECAD marked our evolution into a comprehensive design-build firm. This era saw us take on larger commercial projects and industrial complexes, expanding our technical expertise.

2010s

Building Icons

The past decade cemented our position as industry leaders. Landmark projects like the Ghabour Automotive Complex and Beverly Hills Villas showcased our ability to deliver both aesthetic beauty and functional excellence at scale.

2025

A New Chapter

With Arch. Ibrahim Yousry Harhash at the helm, we’ve transformed into ECADX Group. This evolution expands our capabilities while keeping X Construction as our flagship - combining decades of experience with forward-thinking innovation.
